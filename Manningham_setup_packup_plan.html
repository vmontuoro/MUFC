Manningham United Blues – Pitch Setup & Pack-up Plan

Pettys Reserve · Powerful Owl Park · Timber Ridge Reserve  |  17 Jul → mid-Sep 2026

How duties work

▶ SET UP — mini/mod (U7–U13)

  • First team on a pitch (or when the age group changes)
  • Bring out the goals and corner poles
  • If no line markings, lay yellow flat plates
  • U12/13 only: spare linesman flags in the ref/marshall room

▶ SET UP — full-pitch football (U14+)

  • First full-pitch game of the day brings out the stretcher (one per ground) & the pitch’s corner poles
  • Last full-pitch game puts the stretcher & corner poles away

■ PACK UP

  • Last of an age group on a pitch packs up (U7 not followed by U7, U8/9 not by U8/9, U10–13 not by U10–13)
  • Pack up goals & corner poles; full-pitch last game also the stretcher
  • Return borrowed kit

Every team — always

  • Each team MUST have a marshall (not the Team Manager)
  • Always return borrowed kit
  • Spare whistles, bibs & linesman flags are in a box in the ref room
